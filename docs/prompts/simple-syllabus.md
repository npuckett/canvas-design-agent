## About This Course

This studio course introduces fundamental drawing concepts including line, form, value, and composition. Students develop observational skills through guided exercises and independent projects, working primarily in graphite, charcoal, and ink.

No prior drawing experience is required.

## Materials Needed

- Sketchbook (9×12" minimum)
- Graphite pencil set (2H through 6B)
- Charcoal sticks (vine and compressed)
- India ink and dip pens
- Kneaded eraser and white vinyl eraser
- Drawing board or clipboard

## Course Policies

Attendance is mandatory. Missing more than 3 classes without documentation will lower your final grade by one letter.

Bring all your materials to every class. Studio time is limited and we make the most of it when everyone is prepared.

Your sketchbook is a practice journal — it should show regular work, not just finished pieces. I check sketchbooks every Tuesday at the start of class.
