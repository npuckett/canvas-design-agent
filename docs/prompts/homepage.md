## Welcome Message

Welcome to Creative Writing: Short Fiction! This course is a workshop-based exploration of the short story form. You'll read widely, write regularly, and develop your voice as a fiction writer through peer feedback and revision.

Whether you're a seasoned writer or trying fiction for the first time, this space is built for experimentation. Take risks with your work — that's where the best stories come from.

## Course Modules

Module 1: Elements of Story (Weeks 1–3)
Character, setting, conflict — the building blocks.

Module 2: Point of View & Voice (Weeks 4–6)
Who tells the story and how it sounds.

Module 3: Structure & Revision (Weeks 7–9)
Scene, summary, and the art of cutting.

Module 4: Workshop & Portfolio (Weeks 10–14)
Full workshop cycles and final portfolio assembly.
